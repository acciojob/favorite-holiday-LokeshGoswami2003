Goa


Goa have beaches and many sites to visit , a blend of culture

  1. Palolem and Baga beaches
  2. Basilica of Bom Jesus, a UNESCO World Heritage site
  3. Dudhsagar Waterfalls
  4. Fort Aguada
  5. Anjuna Flea Market
  • Bebinca A traditional Goan layered dessert made with coconut milk, sugar, and eggs
  • Sannas: Steamed rice cakes.
  • Khatkhatem: A mild, yellow Goan curry
